Feedback

HAPPY CUSTOMERS

MyOffice App has worked tremendously well for me. It has always been a challenge for advocates to inform the next date to clients when they call and to update them about the status of their case and to do the same, every time we have to get the file physically to see the ordersheet. MyOffice app has cut short this long procedure and has literally put all my cases into my hand. All I need to do is to open the app, look for the case and there you go, you have the next date and the last status of the case in your hand. Managing cases has never been so easy
Best part about this app is its simplicity. It is so user friendly that my senior, who is running in mid-sixties and is not very tech friendly, is also able to use it easily. “Insert” option is for adding up the cases and “View and Edit” is to look for the case details and make the desired changes in the next date and status of the case.
The developer has really looked into the actual requirements of the advocates and I believe the developer also knows that many advocates face difficult time in dealing with technological changes and thus, has made it ultra user friendly. Not only that the developer has even customized the app for my preferences.
Being a beginner in practice, I was skeptical about the price of the app but its affordability has made it much easier for me to buy it. So it’s not only user friendly but it is friendly to the pocket too.
The best part of MyOffice app, it’s for your lifetime. No renewal charges and no term duration. You don’t need to approach the developer for making the desired changes and you alone can manage the app that too for the rest of your life.
It’s a must have app for all the advocates for adding up comfort and convenience to their hectic professional life by managing their files through MyOffice App.

Rishi Bhushan Jauhari
Advocate, High Court, Allahabad
